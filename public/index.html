Western Canadian Blacksmiths' Guild

The Western Canadian Blacksmiths' Guild promotes blacksmithing in Alberta through events, education, and community for all skill levels.

Established in 1986

“By hammer and hand all arts do stand.” - Motto of the Worshipful Company of Blacksmiths, the ancient blacksmiths' guild of London
We are committed to furthering the awareness and understanding of blacksmithing to the public. We have a shop near Leduc, Alberta where members of our society can come and learn how to forge with experienced smiths.
